Pumpkin Potage

Your meal for the next 400 clear attempts.

Tired of getting one-shot by raidwides? Doing less damage than your teammates? Want to hit a better than grey parse on FFLogs?

Then you need Pumpkin Potage! With amazing stats bonuses (for 30 minutes) like:

  • Determination +8% (Max 72)
  • Vitality +8% (Max 74)
  • Critical Hit +8% (Max 43)
  • EXP Bonus +3%

you'll be sure to clear the toughest content!

Warning: eating Pumpkin Potage only enhances player stats, not skill.

Ingredients

  • 1 Giant Pumpkin
  • 1 Potent Spice
  • 2 Northen Salt
  • 1 Ovibos Milk
  • 1 Alien Onion
  • 1 Ambrosial Water
  • 3 Fire Cluster
  • 3 Water Cluster

Steps

  1. Get a Culinarian to level 90.
  2. Equip the best gear that scrips and money can buy.
  3. Sit down in the middle of Old Sharlayan and use a macro you found online to craft some HQ food.

Congratulations! Now you have some delicious pixelated food that you can eat or sell on the market board for some sweet, sweet gil!
